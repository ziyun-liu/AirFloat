Support

AirFloat is the result of countless hours of work. It is a complete reimplementation of the AirPlay audio protocol. And as such it has been almost four months in the works as a full time project.

AirFloat is made freely available to the jailbreak community as an homage to all the great work being done in the pursuit of making iOS an even greater platform.

If you like AirFloat and like using it, please consider supporting me in the continuous development and maintenance of the app.

Thanks!

Source codes are available through GitHub.
